Tips before you contact

  • 🔍Cross-verify rent across at least 3 sites. Same flat can be listed at different rates depending on broker vs. owner.
  • 🏠NoBroker is owner-direct (no brokerage fee). MagicBricks & 99acres have both broker and owner listings — filter "Owner" to skip brokerage (typically 1 month's rent).
  • 📅Listing age matters. Anything posted >30 days back is likely stale or already rented. Sort by "Newest first" on each site.
  • 📸Check photos + RERA ID. Avoid listings without photos or unverified contact details.
  • 💬Mention "no brokerage" when speaking with owners on NoBroker/99acres to confirm.
  • ⚡For new/upcoming projects, listings will be minimal — check developer/builder website or call the project sales office.
